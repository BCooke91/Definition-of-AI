Welcome to AI

What is AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a term used to describe computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI systems can learn, solve problems, and make decisions.

Why We Need AI

AI helps improve efficiency, enables automation, and allows us to solve complex problems that were previously unsolvable. AI is important because it enables systems to perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence at a fast pace. This includes tasks like problem-solving, decision-making, and learning from data. It can also be useful in healthcare, finance, education, and many other industries.
