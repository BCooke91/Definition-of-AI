Definition of AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a term used to describe computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI systems can learn, solve problems, and make decisions.

Why we need AI?

AI helps improve efficiency, enables automation, and allows us to solve complex problems that were previously unsolvable.

How AI can help us

AI is important because it enables systems to perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence at a fast pace. This includes tasks like problem-solving, decision-making, and learning from data. It can also be useful in healthcare, finance, education, and many other industries.
